Privacy

Ticketwise stores only the Discord IDs and ticket data needed to run support workflows. That includes guild configuration, ticket transcripts, FAQ entries, FAQ candidates, and moderator decisions used for auditability and better future answers.

Ticket questions, conversation context, and relevant FAQs may be sent to Anthropic to generate AI drafts and FAQ candidates. Drafts are generated for moderator review and are not sent to members until a human chooses to send or edit them.
